Winners

Overall Winner

The algorithm for calculating the overall winner is as follows: A hack's final rating is its average normalized rating, where the normalized rating of a hack given by a judge is the judge's rating over the sum of all ratings given by that judge.

Superlative Winners

The algorithm for calculating superlative winners is as follows: A hack recieves two points in its superlative category for each judge that rates it first and one point for each judge that rates it second.
